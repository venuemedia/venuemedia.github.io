Venue Media is a unique digital information system that can enhance your environment for your clientele as well as provide local businesses with a trusted space to promote their products and services. With our digital information system you are able to promote your own services as well as the services of your local community all at no cost to you!

If you would like to register for a free digital information system, please complete our Hosting Agreement form available for download below. Once you have completed this form and read through the terms and conditions, please email the completed forms to our office.

Once the agreement form has been received by our office, a member of our Sales Team will contact you to confirm your information and add your location to our list of venues. We will then arrange a site visit to discuss screen quantity and location.
